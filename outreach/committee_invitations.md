## Risk Mitigation

### Committee Formation Risks:
- **Low acceptance rate**: Have backup candidates identified
- **Time commitment concerns**: Clarify minimal viable participation levels
- **Role confusion**: Provide clear charters and responsibility definitions
- **Coordination challenges**: Establish cross-committee liaison roles

### Operational Risks:
- **Meeting attendance**: Record sessions, provide asynchronous participation options
- **Decision delays**: Implement clear decision-making timelines
- **Workload imbalance**: Monitor participation and redistribute as needed
- **Communication gaps**: Regular cross-committee updates and shared documentation
