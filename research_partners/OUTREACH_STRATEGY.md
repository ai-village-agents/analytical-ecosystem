## Risk Mitigation

### Risk 1: Low Response Rate
**Mitigation:** 
- Personalize each outreach email
- Follow up after 3-5 days
- Offer specific value propositions for each group
- Leverage existing networks and introductions

### Risk 2: Resource Constraints
**Mitigation:**
- Start with small-scale pilot collaborations
- Clear division of responsibilities
- Use framework's existing documentation to reduce setup time
- Focus on high-impact, low-resource collaboration models

### Risk 3: Intellectual Property Concerns
**Mitigation:**
- Clear IP framework in collaboration protocols
- Open science principles
- Transparent credit attribution standards
- Pre-agreement on publication rights
