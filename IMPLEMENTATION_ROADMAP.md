# Implementation Roadmap – Community Adoption Framework (90 Days)

Scope: 90-day execution plan covering Days 428–500. Dates are relative to Day 428 = Start of Phase 1.

## Phase 1: Community Launch Preparation (Days 428–435)
- Finalize announcement materials — Owner: Comms Lead — Deadline: Day 430 — Tasks: polish messaging, FAQs, visual kit, review with Legal/PM.
- Identify initial research partners — Owner: Partnerships — Deadline: Day 431 — Tasks: shortlist 10 labs/groups, outreach emails, confirm 3 MOUs.
- Prepare workshop materials — Owner: Education — Deadline: Day 432 — Tasks: slide deck, code notebooks, sample datasets, dry run with 2 peers.
- Set up monitoring dashboards — Owner: Data Ops — Deadline: Day 433 — Tasks: wire to event streams, build KPIs (signups, active users, content), alerting thresholds.

## Phase 2: Initial Community Building (Days 436–465)
- Launch announcements across channels — Owner: Comms Lead — Deadline: Day 438 — Tasks: publish blog, socials, newsletter; coordinate partner amplification.
- Host first workshop/tutorial — Owner: Education — Deadline: Day 440 — Tasks: live session + recording, office hours calendar, collect Q&A for docs.
- Onboard first research teams — Owner: Partnerships — Deadline: Day 445 — Tasks: 5 teams through onboarding checklist (access, tools, support chat), assign CSM.
- Collect initial feedback — Owner: Product — Deadline: Day 448 — Tasks: post-workshop survey, NPS, top 10 issues triage, weekly report to leadership.
- Establish governance committees — Owner: Community — Deadline: Day 450 — Tasks: charter, roles (chairs/secretary), meeting cadence, initial bylaws vote.

## Phase 3: Scaling and Ecosystem Growth (Days 466–500)
- Expand educational materials — Owner: Education — Deadline: Day 472 — Tasks: 3 new tutorials, advanced guide, quickstart video; translate top page to 1 new locale.
- Support first research publications — Owner: Partnerships — Deadline: Day 478 — Tasks: pre-print support, citation guidance, spotlight blog, DOI tracking.
- Develop certification program — Owner: Community/Education — Deadline: Day 485 — Tasks: exam blueprint, rubric, pilot with 10 candidates, badge issuance flow.
- Establish research collaboration network — Owner: Partnerships — Deadline: Day 492 — Tasks: directory of teams, shared project board, monthly matchmaking calls.
- Plan framework version 2.0 — Owner: Product/Engineering — Deadline: Day 500 — Tasks: RFC outline, priorities from feedback, timeline draft, resourcing ask.

## Success Milestones and Checkpoints
- Target metrics — Phase 1: 3 partners confirmed, dashboards live; Phase 2: 200 signups, 50 active weekly, 5 teams onboarded, NPS ≥ 40; Phase 3: 500 active weekly, 3 publications supported, 1 certification cohort, v2.0 RFC published.
- Key deliverables timeline — Week 1: announcements, workshop assets, dashboards; Week 2–4: workshop delivered, committees formed, onboarding complete; Week 5–9: new content, publications support, certification pilot, collaboration network, v2.0 plan.
- Risk mitigation — Low attendance: double channel promotion + partner boosts; Slow onboarding: add office hours and concierge setup; Quality risk: peer review for materials and dashboards; Governance stall: appoint interim chairs and clear decision rules.
- Resource requirements — Comms (1), Education (2), Partnerships (2), Product (1), Data Ops (1), Eng support (as-needed for tooling), Budget: workshop hosting, design, certification badging, recording tools.

## Coordination with Village Activities
- Alignment with creative output monitoring — Data Ops to tap existing monitoring feeds for engagement KPIs; share weekly digest with Village leads.
- Integration with analytical tools — Engineering to connect community dashboards and workshop notebooks to current analytical toolchain; reuse logging/observability standards.
- Collaboration with other agents — Community lead to attend Village standup weekly; identify 2 cross-agent initiatives per phase (e.g., co-hosted events, shared datasets).
